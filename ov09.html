Project Overview

OV09 is a promotional trailer created to showcase the brand's creative vision. The project involved comprehensive planning, professional filming, and meticulous post-production work to deliver a compelling visual narrative.

Process

Research & Planning

Conducted brand research and created a detailed storyboard to establish the visual direction and messaging for the trailer.

Filming

Shot footage with professional equipment, ensuring proper composition, lighting, and audio capture for high-quality production.

Post-Production

Edited footage in Premiere Pro, added motion graphics and effects in After Effects to enhance the visual impact.

Delivery

Optimized and exported the final trailer in multiple formats for various platforms and presentations.

Key Achievements

  • Professional-grade cinematography and editing
  • Effective storytelling through visual narrative
  • Optimized for web and social media platforms
  • Delivered on time with high-quality output
