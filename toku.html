Toku Studios

Brand identity, visual direction and research.

Role

Brand Designer, Researcher

Tools

Figma, Adobe Creative Suite

Outcome

Complete brand system

Timeline

6 weeks research & design

Project Overview

Toku Studios is a creative brand that required a comprehensive identity system. The project involved extensive market research, competitive analysis, and iterative design to establish a strong visual presence that aligns with the studio's creative vision and values.

Process

Research

Conducted market research and competitor analysis to understand the creative industry landscape and identify opportunities for differentiation.

Strategy

Developed brand strategy, messaging framework, and visual direction based on insights and client goals.

Design

Created comprehensive brand guidelines including logo, color palette, typography, and design patterns in Figma.

Refinement

Iterated on designs based on feedback and applied the brand system across multiple touchpoints.

Brand Elements

  • Logo design with multiple variations
  • Color palette with primary and accent colors
  • Typography system for web and print
  • Design patterns and component library
  • Brand guidelines documentation
  • Application examples for various media
